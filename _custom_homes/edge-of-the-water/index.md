Entertaining is a big focus and the home does so admirably. Its five guest suites and five full and three 1/2 baths accommodate the owner’s large family and their special guests.

The expansive great room is open to the Kitchen and Dining space. The dining room features a table for 12 and disappearing corner-less sliding glass panels that makes dining feel like an outdoor event. The first level features the Master Suite and a Guest Suite, as well as a his and hers Study and Utility Room. The home features a pair of garages for adequate car storage, as well as any water craft.

The outdoor area features an outdoor kitchen and a large two-story seating area with a stone fireplace. There is a dramatic pool, pergola, and fire pit as well leading to the beach beyond.

The second floor is accessed via a dramatic floating staircase and elevator. It has an additional three Guest Suites, Wet Bar, Game Room and even an Exercise Room.

**It is easy to see why this home is so loved by it’s owners.**
